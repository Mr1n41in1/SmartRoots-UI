Getting Started

Welcome to SmartRoots, your comprehensive agricultural monitoring solution. This guide will help you navigate through the platform and make the most of its features.

To begin monitoring your fields, navigate to the Dashboard where you can view real-time data, maps, and recommendations. Use the sidebar to access different sections of the application.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often is the data updated?

Data is updated in real-time from field sensors. You can adjust the update frequency in Settings according to your preference.

How do I interpret the NDVI values?

NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index) values range from -1 to 1. Higher values (0.3-1) indicate healthy vegetation, while lower values suggest stressed or sparse vegetation.

Can I export my data for analysis?

Yes, you can export your data in CSV format from the Previous Records section. This allows you to perform further analysis using external tools.

How accurate are the predictions?

Our predictions are based on historical data, current conditions, and advanced machine learning algorithms. While highly accurate, they should be used as guidance alongside your expertise.
